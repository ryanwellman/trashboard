Required Addition to Monitoring Agreement

As you may or may not know, the City of Tacoma requires an annual alarm device license (permit) for each alarm system and also charges fines for false alarms. According to the Monitoring Agreement Section 3.1 the customer is required to obtain any necessary permit(s) and it is the customer's sole responsibility, to obtain, pay for, and maintain all necessary permit(s); also according to Section 8.3 all fines, penalties or charges for false alarms or signals shall be the sole responsibility of customer to pay.

Because the City of Tacoma will invoice Protect America, Inc. the fees for the annual alarm device license (permit) plus any false alarms, SIX DOLLARS ($6.00) per month will be added to the monitoring rate listed on your monitoring agreement.

This additional monthly fee will cover the yearly alarm device license (permit) and any false alarms assessed.
