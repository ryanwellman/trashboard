Addendum to Monitoring Agreement

As you may or may not know, the Detroit Police Department requires private, third party, on-site alarm verification of all home security burglar alarm activations BEFORE the Detroit Police Department will dispatch a unit to the location. You are obligated to comply with this policy.

Under this policy, when a home security burglar alarm is activated, the monitoring station must first call a third-party, contracted, guard service to be dispatched to the alarm location. The guard service will be required to check the perimeter of the property, and look for signs of forced entry. If there are no signs of forced entry found, then the guard service will contact the monitoring station, and prepare a report. If signs of forced entry are found, or if the guard service observes an emergency condition, the guard service will remain at the property and contact the monitoring station so that a Police unit can be dispatched to the location. This procedure does not apply to panic, robbery, duress or other types of hold-up alarms. Police units will continue to respond to the panic types of alarms as usual.

Protect America, Inc. has negotiated a special rate with a local verified response company for this required service. For this required service, FIVE DOLLARS ($5.00) per month will be added to the monitoring rate listed on your monitoring agreement. Any additional responses over one (1) on-site responses per year will be billed to you at FOURTY-FIVE ($45.00) per response. The alarm response company requires an additional $30.00 if their responder is needed to provide any service beyond the normal alarm response. You will be invoiced this fee for each occurrence.

The fees stated in this Addendum are subject to change, and the local verified Response Company may charge fees for additional services. If the local verified Response Company increases its fees or charges additional fees, then you agree to pay such increase and/or additional fees upon written request from Protect America, Inc. You are responsible for all costs or fees imposed by the guard service or private response company.

PROTECT AMERICA SHALL HAVE NO LIABILITY WHATSOEVER FOR OR ARISING OUT OF THE GUARD SERVICE OR PRIVATE RESPONSE COMPANY OR FOR THE FAILURE TO COMPLY IN ANY WAY WITH THE STATUES OR ORDINANCES OF THE CITY OF DETROIT.
