Burglar Alarm Registration for Palm Beach County

Per the Palm Beach County Ordinance Sec. 16-54, "It shall be a violation of this article to operate an alarm system without a valid alarm permit".

There is a non-refundable registration fee of $25.00. Registrations are renewed on an annual basis at a cost of $25.00.

With an alarm permit you will receive two free false alarms within a calendar year, each subsequent alarm will start at $50.00 and will increase in increment of $25.00 thereafter.

I, the alarm user, understand that I will receive a Palm Beach County Burglar Alarm Permit application with my system.

I, the alarm user, understand that my account will be on non-dispatch/log-only until I provide Protect America, Inc. proof that I have registered my alarm system. This means in the event of an alarm, the monitoring company will not request police dispatch.
