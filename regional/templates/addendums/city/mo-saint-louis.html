St. Louis, Missouri

Electrical Permit / Alarm Permit Addendum

I/we the undersigned understand that the operations of any/all alarm systems are governed by the City of St. Louis Ordinances and according to these, both an electrical permit and an alarm permit are required to be obtained before the installation of an alarm system. The total fee for a residential address is One Hundred and Ten Dollars ($110.00) and for a non-residential/commercial address is One Hundred and Thirty-Five Dollars ($135.00).

I/we the undersigned understand and agree to submit an upfront payment of Eighty-Five Dollars ($85.00) to Protect America, Inc. for an Electrical Permit, which will be acquired by Protect America, Inc. and issued by the City of St. Louis Electrical Department.

I/we also understand that once the alarm system is installed, it is my/our, the Subscriber’s responsibility to call the St. Louis Electrical Department, (314) 622-3325, between the hours of 8:00 a.m. – 9:00a.m. Monday- Friday and schedule an appointment time for the inspection of the alarm system. The alarm system SHALL be inspected within six (6) months of the date the electrical permit is issued. It is a requirement per the City of St. Louis Electrical Department that the alarm system be inspected within six months.

I/we the Subscriber will be issued an Alarm Permit Number after the Electrical Permit has been approved and issued. The St. Louis Alarm Registration and False Alarm Management (ARFAM) Services will issue the Alarm Permit Number. I/we also understand that the police department will not be dispatched if an Alarm Permit Number for the alarm system installed is not issued or has been suspended due to non-payment of the yearly registration fee or any false alarm fines.

I/We, the undersigned, per my/our agreement fully understand what protection this system will provide and the specific areas, openings and items it will protect. I/We will receive instructions on how to operate this system. I/We understand that I/we must register this alarm with the St. Louis Alarm ARFAM Services; through Protect America, Inc; who will then assign me/us an "Alarm Permit Number" before I/we can operate this system, and that misuse, neglect or other conditions that result in false alarms will result in fines imposed against me/us by the City of St. Louis.

The cost of the Alarm Permit is Twenty-Five Dollars ($25.00) for residential addresses and Fifty Dollars ($50.00) for non-residential/commercial addresses. The permit renews annually. I/we the undersigned understand and agree to submit the Twenty-Five Dollars ($25.00) residential or the Fifty Dollars ($50.00) non-residential/commercial alarm permit fee upfront to Protect America, Inc. whom will register my/our alarm system with St. Louis ARFAM Services after the Electrical Permit has been issued. I/we the undersigned also understand and agree to submit the annual alarm renewal fee when requested by Protect America, Inc.

I/we understand that if I/we move to another address within the City of St. Louis the Electrical and Alarm Permits will be invalid and a new electrical and alarm permit has to be applied for at a total cost of One Hundred and Ten Dollars ($110.00) for residential addresses and One Hundred and Thirty-Five Dollars ($135.00) for commercial addresses.

The operations of any/all alarm systems are governed by the City of St. Louis Ordinances and violations of the provisions of these ordinances can result in a minimum fine of not less than Twenty-Five Dollars ($25.00) plus court costs and not more than Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) plus court costs and/or 30 days imprisonment.

*** Your account will be placed on log only and will remain on log only until we receive your completed Electrical Permit / Alarm Registration Information Form. *** This means in the event of an alarm, the monitoring company will not request police dispatch.
